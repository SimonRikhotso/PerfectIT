Student Referral Program

PerfectIT rewards students who help grow the learning community.

If you refer a student who successfully enrolls in a module, you receive a reward.

How it works

  • Refer a friend who needs programming support
  • They register for a PerfectIT module
  • Your reward is activated automatically

Rewards

  • Free additional support session OR
  • Discount on your next module fee

This system encourages collaboration and helps build a strong learning network among students.

Example

If Student A refers Student B and Student B enrolls, Student A becomes eligible for a referral reward.
