PERFECTIT HOME ADVERT
=====================

FINAL FILE USED BY THE WEBSITE
------------------------------
Export your completed advert as:

    perfectit-home-advert.mp4

Place it inside this folder:

    videos/home-advert/perfectit-home-advert.mp4

Then open data/videos.json and change the home advert's:

    "enabled": false

to:

    "enabled": true

The website will:
- autoplay the advert silently where the browser permits it;
- invite visitors to select “Play with sound”;
- keep the complete advert looping while the Home page remains open;
- pause when the browser tab is hidden and resume when the visitor returns.

EDITING FOLDERS
---------------
clips/         Put your short raw teaching and supporting clips here.
audio/         Put narration and background music here.
project-files/ Put your CapCut, Clipchamp or other editor project here.

Recommended final video:
- MP4 using H.264 video and AAC audio
- 1920 x 1080 or 1280 x 720
- 16:9 landscape
- 30 to 40 seconds
- preferably below 15 MB for website performance

A full production guide is available at the project root:

    PERFECTIT-HOME-ADVERT-PRODUCTION-GUIDE.txt
